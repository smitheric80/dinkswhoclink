A Community for Couples Without Kids

DINKs Who Clink is an intimate, invite-only community for Southern California couples who choose a child-free lifestyle. We believe in the power of shared experiences—whether it's discovering a hidden wine country gem, hosting an elegant dinner party, or simply gathering with like-minded friends.

This isn't a networking group or a dating service. This is about genuine connection, meaningful friendships, and building a community where every couple contributes and every gathering matters.

We're selective not to exclude, but to ensure that every member values quality over quantity, giving over taking, and authentic connection over surface-level socializing.

How Membership Works

1

Get Invited

Membership begins with an invitation from a current member. This ensures our community grows thoughtfully through trusted connections.

2

Trial Period

Attend your first two events as a trial member. This gives you a chance to experience the community and for members to get to know you.

3

Community Approval

After your trial events, current members share their feedback. We're looking for couples who are warm, engaged, and ready to contribute.

4

Host & Connect

As a full member, you'll host your first event within 30 days. From intimate dinners to weekend getaways, you help shape our community.
